Problem

AirQuail monitors environment condition and polution level for businesses and smart home platforms.

Using an assortment of sensors and pre-processing techniques, readings are collected and pushed onto our servers for analysis. As a result, we make meaningful inferences about the thin air you can neither smell or see.

Smart-city and smart-home applications can leverage our crowd-sourced data to drive automated interaction.
